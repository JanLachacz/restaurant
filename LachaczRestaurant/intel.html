About Us

Welcome to Mario's Pizzeria! We have been serving our neighborhood delicious, handmade pizza for over 25 years. Every pie is made fresh using our family's secret dough recipe and the finest toppings. Whether you are dining in or taking out, we promise a meal you will never forget!

We are proud to be a family owned and operated restaurant. Our goal is simple — great food, great service, and great memories.

Why Choose Us?

  • Family recipes passed down for generations
  • Dine in, carry out, and catering available
  • Friendly staff and cozy atmosphere
